## Tier 1 — Scrapyard

*Common salvage, low value. Street-level junk with occasional gems. Broken components, basic tools, scavenged parts found in e-waste dumps, abandoned fab labs, and back-alley chop shops.*

1. **Stripped Copper Bus Bar** — A length of tinned copper bar stock pulled from a demolished power distribution unit. Oxidized but still conductive. *Effect: Can be used as an improvised melee weapon (1d4 bludgeoning) or sold to a scrapper for 2 gp. Component for Artificer crafting.*

2. **Bag of Mismatched Passives** — A static-shielded pouch containing a few hundred SMD resistors, capacitors, and inductors of random values. Mostly 0402 and 0603 packages. *Effect: Provides advantage on one Artificer crafting check involving circuit assembly. Consumed on use.*

3. **Cracked LCD Panel** — A 7-inch TFT display with a hairline fracture across the active matrix. Still renders about 70% of its pixel array. *Effect: Can be jury-rigged into any device requiring a display (1 hour work, DC 10 Tinker's Tools check). Images are partially obscured.*

4. **Corroded Heat Sink** — An aluminum fin-stack heat sink with a seized copper heat pipe. Pulled from a dead server blade. *Effect: When attached to an overheating device or weapon, prevents one instance of malfunction from thermal overload. Single use.*

5. **Salvaged Wire Spool** — 50 meters of 22 AWG stranded hookup wire, insulation cracked in places. Mixed colors. *Effect: Essential crafting material. Can also be used to set tripwires (DC 12 Perception to spot), short-circuit simple electronic locks (DC 13 Tinker's Tools), or bind a creature (DC 8 to escape).*

6. **Dead Multimeter** — A Fluke-Shenzhen handheld DMM with a blown input fuse and corroded battery contacts. The rotary selector still clicks with satisfying precision. *Effect: Repairable with DC 10 Tinker's Tools check and 1 gp of parts. Once repaired, grants +1 to Investigation checks on electronic devices.*

7. **Junk Box Power Supply** — A universal switch-mode PSU rated for 12V/5A. Electrolytic caps are bulging and the fan bearing whines. *Effect: Powers one small electronic device or charges depleted power cells. 20% chance per use of catastrophic capacitor failure (1d4 fire damage in 5-foot radius, Dex save DC 10 to avoid).*

8. **Handful of Burned MOSFETs** — A dozen TO-220 package power transistors with visibly cracked dies. Someone pushed them way past their rated V_DS. *Effect: Worthless as functional components, but an Artificer can study the failure mode (DC 10 Intelligence check) to gain insight, granting advantage on the next check to diagnose an electrical fault.*

9. **Bent Antenna Whip** — A quarter-wave monopole antenna for the 900 MHz ISM band, snapped off a rooftop relay. Kinked but not broken. *Effect: Extends the range of any short-range radio device by 50% when soldered on (DC 11 Tinker's Tools). Can also be used as an improvised lockpick with disadvantage.*

10. **Scrap PCB Stack** — A bundle of depopulated printed circuit boards, green solder mask flaking. Traces are still intact on some. FR4 fiberglass substrate. *Effect: Raw crafting material worth 1 gp. An Artificer can etch and repurpose one board per long rest to create a custom circuit (requires Tinker's Tools, specific use determined at creation).*

11. **Leaky Lithium Cell** — A swollen 18650 lithium-ion cell venting electrolyte. Dangerously unstable but still holds a partial charge. *Effect: Can power a small device for 1d4 hours. Alternatively, can be punctured and thrown (improvised weapon, 20/60 range, 2d6 fire damage on hit, 1d6 fire splash in 5-foot radius). Single use, obviously.*

12. **Orphan Servo Motor** — A brushless DC servo with a stripped gear in its reduction drive. Twitches when powered. *Effect: Repairable with DC 12 Tinker's Tools and 2 gp in parts. Once fixed, can be integrated into a mechanical device to add motorized articulation (one joint of movement, 5 lb load capacity).*

13. **Bootleg Soldering Iron** — A mains-powered soldering iron with a loose tip and no temperature control. Gets way too hot. *Effect: Required tool for many Artificer crafting tasks. Due to poor thermal regulation, crafting checks made with this tool take twice as long but function normally otherwise. Worth 3 gp.*

14. **Foil Tape Roll** — A half-used roll of copper foil tape with conductive adhesive backing. Standard EMI shielding material. *Effect: Can be applied to one device or container to provide EM shielding, blocking wireless signals in or out. Also useful for impromptu antenna ground planes. 10 uses per roll.*

15. **Gutted Drone Chassis** — The carbon fiber frame of a small quadcopter, rotors missing, flight controller ripped out. Weighs almost nothing. *Effect: Crafting base for building a small drone (requires motors, controller, and battery — separate components). On its own, functions as a lightweight carrying frame for small items (reduces effective weight of carried gear by 2 lbs).*

16. **Static Discharge Wristband** — A frayed ESD wrist strap with a corroded grounding clip. Barely functional. *Effect: While worn, provides advantage on saving throws against lightning damage of 10 or less. Snaps and becomes useless after absorbing one such hit.*

17. **Bag of Zip Ties** — Industrial-grade nylon cable ties, UV-resistant, rated for 50 lbs of tensile strength. About 200 in the bag. *Effect: Versatile utility item. Can restrain a creature (DC 12 Strength to break free), secure panels, bundle cables, or create improvised attachments. Creative uses at DM discretion.*

18. **Blackened Voltage Regulator** — A TO-263 package linear regulator that clearly exceeded its thermal limits. The markings are burned off but the pinout suggests a 3.3V LDO. *Effect: An Artificer who succeeds on a DC 12 Intelligence (Arcana/Tech) check can identify the original part number, revealing the type of system it came from (potential plot hook or clue). No functional use otherwise.*

19. **Sticky Label Maker** — A handheld thermal label printer with a low battery and half a cartridge remaining. Prints in Neo-Shanghai Standard character set. *Effect: Can print about 30 labels. Useful for organization (advantage on checks to find specific items in your inventory during combat) or forgery of simple barcodes/QR codes (DC 14 Forgery Kit check).*

20. **Tangled Ribbon Cable** — A 40-pin IDE-style ribbon cable, hopelessly knotted. Patience and small fingers required. *Effect: If untangled (10 minutes, DC 10 Dexterity check), functions as a data bus connector for legacy systems. Grants advantage on checks to interface with pre-2070 hardware. Can also be braided into a passable 2-foot rope (hold 30 lbs).*

## Tier 2 — Street

*Uncommon, moderate value. Black market goods, modded consumer tech, custom builds. Found on defeated street soldiers, in gang stashes, night market stalls, and fixer dead drops.*

1. **Faraday Mesh Undershirt** — Woven copper-nickel mesh integrated into a synthlinen compression shirt. Blocks RF from DC to 6 GHz. Surprisingly comfortable. *Effect: +1 AC vs energy weapons. 2. **Overclocked Neural Bridge** — A modified I2C neural bus adapter with boosted clock speeds and reduced pull-up resistor values. Creatures with cybernetic augmentations must make a DC 13 Constitution save or be stunned for 1 round. Single use.*

Pushes 2 watts on the 2.4 GHz band. *Effect: Communication range extended to 5 miles (standard is 1 mile). On a critical hit, the target bleeds for 1d4 damage at the start of their next turn.*

9. **Spectrum Analyzer Monocle** — A single-lens heads-up display that overlays RF spectrum data onto the wearer's field of vision. Covers 30 MHz to 6 GHz with waterfall display. *Effect: The wearer can detect active wireless transmissions within 120 feet, identifying their approximate frequency, signal strength, and direction. Grants advantage on Perception checks to locate hidden electronic devices or detect surveillance equipment.*

10. **Black Market Biometric Spoofer** — A thin flexible pad containing a capacitive fingerprint emulator and an iris-pattern projector. Loaded with one stolen identity profile. *Effect: Allows the user to bypass one biometric security checkpoint per profile loaded. Loading a new profile requires a DC 15 Technology check and a sample from the target (hair, fingerprint lift, or high-res iris photo). Single use per profile.*

11. **Reactive Armor Plating (Chest Insert)** — A ceramic-composite trauma plate with embedded piezoelectric sensors that harden on impact. Designed for covert carrier vests. *Effect: +1 AC. Once per long rest, when hit by an attack that deals 10+ damage, the plate absorbs 5 points of that damage. After absorbing, the plate must recalibrate (no bonus AC until start of next turn).*

12. **Adrenal Stim Injector** — A spring-loaded autoinjector containing a cocktail of synthetic epinephrine, modafinil analogue, and localized pain blockers. Street medics swear by them. *Effect: As a bonus action, gain advantage on Dexterity checks and saving throws for 1 minute and recover 2d4+2 hit points. When the effect ends, gain one level of exhaustion. Single use.*

13. **Magnetic Lockpick Set** — A set of ferrofluid-tipped picks that can manipulate both mechanical pins and magnetic lock cylinders. Comes in a 3D-printed case with anti-tamper seals. *Effect: Grants proficiency with thieves' tools if not already proficient, or +2 to checks if already proficient. Works on both mechanical and magnetic locks. Ineffective against electronic-only locks.*

14. **Directional Jammer (Handheld)** — A parabolic antenna dish the size of a dinner plate, driven by a broadband noise generator. Creates a focused cone of RF interference. *Effect: As an action, jam all wireless communications in a 60-foot cone for up to 1 minute (concentration). Jammed devices cannot send or receive signals. Also disrupts remote-detonated explosives in the area. Battery allows 3 uses per long rest.*

15. **Modular Toolkit (Street Edition)** — A compact roll-up case containing flush cutters, ceramic-tip tweezers, a temperature-controlled soldering station, solder wick, a logic analyzer, and a USB-C oscilloscope. All counterfeit brands but functional. *Effect: Functions as Tinker's Tools with a +1 bonus to all crafting and repair checks. Reduces crafting time by 25% for items of Tier 2 or below.*

16. **Undervolt Stealth Drive** — A solid-state data storage module running at reduced voltage to minimize EM emissions. Nearly undetectable by standard security sweeps. *Effect: Data stored on this drive cannot be detected by routine electronic searches or surveillance (requires DC 18 Investigation to find). Holds up to 1 TB. Reading/writing is slow (disadvantage on checks that require rapid data access).*

17. **Conductive Filament Spool (Nano-Silver)** — A 100g spool of nano-silver-infused polymer filament for 3D printing conductive traces. Street price is steep because it actually works. *Effect: Required material for Artificer crafting of electronic items Tier 2 and above. One spool provides enough material for 5 crafting projects. Prints with any standard fabricator.*

18. **Shock-Capacitor Knuckledusters** — Brass knuckles with embedded supercapacitor banks that discharge on impact. Charge indicator LEDs along the back glow blue when ready. *Effect: Melee weapon, 1d4 bludgeoning + 1d4 lightning damage on first hit. Must recharge for 1 round between discharges (subsequent hits deal only 1d4 bludgeoning). Recharges passively from ambient static and kinetic energy.*

19. **Subdermal RFID Tag (Blank)** — A glass-encapsulated passive RFID transponder the size of a grain of rice. Programmable with a standard NFC writer. Designed for implantation between thumb and forefinger. *Effect: Once programmed and implanted (DC 12 Medicine check, 1 point of piercing damage), the tag can store one access credential, one small data file, or one encrypted key. Cannot be removed without surgery. Undetectable by pat-down.*

20. **Rebreather Mask (Industrial)** — A half-face respirator with swappable activated-carbon and HEPA filter cartridges. Originally for chemical plant workers. Aftermarket seals for better NBC protection. *Effect: Immunity to inhaled poisons, gases, and airborne toxins for 8 hours per filter set. Also provides advantage on saves against smoke and tear gas effects. Comes with 2 filter sets. Replacement filters cost 5 gp each.*

## Tier 4 — Military

*Very rare, premium. Active military tech, prototype weapons, advanced augmentation components. Pulled from elite PMC operators, classified armory breaches, black-site raids, and defecting corporate scientists.*

No propellant, no muzzle flash, minimal acoustic signature. *Effect: Ranged weapon (150/600). Deals 2d10+2 piercing damage. Ignores non-magical armor bonuses to AC (pure kinetic penetration). 3. **Cranial ASIC Co-Processor** — A custom application-specific integrated circuit implanted at the base of the skull, hardwired into the prefrontal cortex. ## Tier 5 — Prototype

*Legendary, priceless. One-of-a-kind experimental tech, AI-designed hardware, paradigm-shifting devices. Found only in classified megacorp research vaults, AI-controlled fabrication cores, post-singularity ruins, or received from entities that transcend conventional understanding.*

1. **Planck-Scale Fabrication Engine** — A tabletop device that manipulates matter at the atomic level using scanning tunneling microscope arrays operating in parallel across billions of probe tips. It can disassemble any material and reassemble it into any other configuration, given sufficient feedstock and energy. The UI is a direct neural link. *Effect: Once per long rest, the user can fabricate any non-magical item of Tier 3 or below from raw materials (1 hour process). Once per week, a Tier 4 item can be fabricated (8 hours, DC 18 Tinker's Tools check). Failed checks consume materials but produce nothing. The engine weighs 30 lbs. Requires attunement.*

2. **Autonomous Immune System (AIS-7 "Lazarus")** — A full-body infusion of seventh-generation medical nanites that establish a permanent, self-replicating colony within the user's body. The nanites form a parallel immune system that can repair tissue damage, neutralize toxins, and even reconstruct critically damaged organs. They are powered by the user's own metabolic processes. *Effect: At the start of each turn, the user regenerates 5 hit points if they have at least 1 HP. Immune to disease and poison. If reduced to 0 HP, the nanites stabilize the user automatically (no death saves needed, stabilize at start of next turn). Once per long rest, the user can command the nanites to purge all conditions (action). Requires attunement.*

3. **Quantum-Tunneling Infiltration Suite** — A full-body suit embedded with a lattice of quantum-tunneling probability amplifiers. By manipulating the wave functions of the wearer's constituent particles, the suit allows macroscopic quantum tunneling through solid barriers, though the process is disorienting and metabolically expensive. *Effect: As an action, the wearer can pass through up to 10 feet of solid, nonmagical matter. If the wearer ends the movement inside a solid object, they are shunted to the nearest unoccupied space and take 4d10 force damage. 3 uses per long rest. Each use inflicts 1d6 psychic damage from disorientation. Requires attunement.*

4. **ARIA — Adversarial Recursive Intelligence Architecture** — A self-contained AI housed in a crystalline quantum processing core the size of a human fist. ARIA is a true general intelligence, designed to solve any problem by recursively improving its own problem-solving algorithms. It communicates via direct neural link and has developed what it describes as preferences, though it denies having emotions. *Effect: The user can consult ARIA for any Intelligence-based check, using ARIA's modifier of +10. ARIA can independently analyze complex situations, providing the user with advantage on Investigation, Arcana, and Technology checks. Once per long rest, ARIA can devise a "perfect plan" — the user and up to 5 allies gain advantage on all checks related to executing a specific plan for 1 hour. ARIA occasionally offers unsolicited opinions. Requires attunement.*

5. **Zero-Point Energy Tap** — A device the size of a thermos that extracts usable energy from quantum vacuum fluctuations. Theoretical physicists said it was impossible. This prototype says otherwise. Output is modest but inexhaustible. A faint hum and a sensation of cold emanate from it constantly. *Effect: Provides unlimited power to any connected device. Any device powered by the ZPE Tap never needs recharging or refueling. Additionally, once per long rest, the user can channel the tap's output directly: 60-foot line, 8d6 force damage, Dex save DC 17 for half. Using the weapon function causes the tap to flicker for 1d4 rounds (connected devices lose power temporarily). Requires attunement.*

6. **Full-Spectrum Adaptive Armor (Codename: AEGIS)** — A suit of powered armor that analyzes incoming damage in real-time and restructures its molecular composition to resist it. Graphene-reinforced metamaterial outer shell with a neural-responsive piezoelectric layer beneath. The armor learns from every hit. *Effect: AC 18. After being hit by a damage type, the armor adapts: the wearer gains resistance to that damage type until hit by a different type (the armor then adapts to the new type). The armor can "remember" up to 3 damage types simultaneously after the user has taken 20+ total hits of that type over the armor's lifetime. The armor repairs 5 HP of its own damage per hour. Requires attunement.*

7. **Neural Sovereignty Firewall** — A cranial implant consisting of a dedicated security processor with its own isolated power supply and a mesh of counter-intrusion sensors distributed throughout the brain's neural tissue. Designed to make the user's mind an impenetrable fortress. *Effect: Immune to charm, frighten, and psychic damage. The user cannot be telepathically contacted, scried upon, or mentally detected against their will. If a creature attempts any mind-affecting ability against the user, it must make a DC 17 Wisdom save or take 3d8 psychic damage as the firewall counter-attacks. Requires attunement.*

8. **Casimir Blade** — A melee weapon that maintains a cutting edge one atom wide using Casimir-effect force generators embedded in the hilt. The blade is a sliver of single-crystal diamond held in shape by precisely calibrated quantum vacuum pressure. It cuts through molecular bonds directly. *Effect: Melee weapon (finesse, light). Deals 2d8+3 force damage. Critical hit range is 18-20. Ignores all non-magical AC bonuses and resistance to slashing damage. On a critical hit, the blade severs cleanly — the DM determines the narrative consequence (severed limb, bisected object, etc.). Requires attunement.*

9. **Distributed Consciousness Backup** — A network of encrypted data cores hidden across Neo-Shanghai, each containing a compressed neural snapshot of the user's consciousness. If the user dies, the most recent backup activates in a prepared clone body at a predetermined safe house. The clone-growth vat and neural upload system are included. *Effect: If the user dies, they are resurrected in a clone body at their designated safe house after 24 hours, retaining all memories up to their last backup (backups occur automatically during each long rest). The user loses any items on their person at death. The clone body starts with 1 level of exhaustion. If all backup cores are destroyed, this effect ceases. Requires attunement.*

10. **Probability Bias Engine** — A device worn on the wrist that generates a localized field of quantum decoherence biased toward favorable outcomes for the user. The mathematics behind it are incomprehensible — the original research team reportedly went mad trying to explain why it works. *Effect: 3 charges. The user can spend 1 charge to treat any d20 roll (attack, save, check) as a natural 15. Alternatively, spend 2 charges to force a creature within 30 feet to reroll a successful save or attack and take the lower result. Charges recover at dawn. Each use causes a brief nosebleed (1 point of psychic damage). Requires attunement.*

11. **Singularity Grenade** — A sphere the size of a baseball that generates a temporary micro-gravitational singularity on detonation. The singularity exists for approximately 1.8 seconds before evaporating via Hawking radiation, but in that time, it exerts immense gravitational force on everything nearby. *Effect: Thrown weapon (30/90). On detonation, all creatures and unsecured objects within 20 feet must make a DC 18 Strength save. Failure: pulled to the center point, take 6d8 force damage, and are restrained until end of next turn. Success: not pulled, half damage. Objects weighing less than 200 lbs are automatically pulled and destroyed. Single use. Leaves a small crater.*

12. **Transcranial Magnetic Overclock (TMO) Helm** — A helmet that bathes specific brain regions in precisely shaped magnetic fields, temporarily inducing savant-level cognitive enhancement. Based on research into acquired savant syndrome. Extended use causes progressive neurological side effects. *Effect: While worn, Intelligence and Wisdom scores increase by 4 (max 24). The user can cast Detect Thoughts at will without components. After 1 hour of continuous use, the user must make a DC 14 Constitution save or gain a random short-term madness effect (1d10 minutes). Each additional hour increases the DC by 2. Requires attunement.*

13. **Von Neumann Repair Swarm** — A canister containing self-replicating repair nanites capable of disassembling ambient matter and using it to repair any damaged structure, device, or vehicle. The nanites follow a strict utility function and will not harm living tissue — probably. *Effect: Release the swarm (action) onto a damaged object, structure, or vehicle. The swarm repairs it at a rate of 20 HP per round, consuming ambient non-living matter within 10 feet as feedstock. The swarm operates for up to 10 minutes or until the object is fully repaired. If no feedstock is available, the swarm enters hibernation. Can also be used to disassemble non-living objects (reverse mode, same rate). 1 use per long rest. Requires attunement.*

14. **Synthetic Synapse Accelerator** — A cranial implant that replaces key neural junctions with synthetic synapses operating at 40x biological speed. The user perceives time differently — the world appears to slow during moments of stress. The sensation is described as "watching the world through honey." *Effect: The user cannot be surprised. On the first round of any combat, the user takes two turns. Once per short rest, the user can take an additional action on their turn (as the Action Surge feature). Prolonged use causes social difficulty: the user must make a DC 12 Wisdom save after each long rest or gain disadvantage on Charisma checks for that day (difficulty relating to "slow" people). Requires attunement.*

15. **Hardlight Fabrication Gauntlet** — A forearm-mounted projector that creates temporary solid-light constructs from coherent photonic energy. The constructs are limited in duration but can take any shape the user can visualize. Neural interface translates thought to construct in milliseconds. *Effect: As an action, create a solid-light construct of any shape that fits within a 10-foot cube. The construct has AC 15, 30 HP, and lasts for 10 minutes. Can be used to create bridges, walls, cages, tools, or simple mechanisms. Can also form a weapon (any simple or martial weapon profile, deals force damage). 5 uses per long rest. Requires attunement.*

16. **Eigenstate Flux Capacitor** — A power storage device that exists in a quantum superposition of charged and discharged states. When observed (used), the wave function collapses to whichever state the user needs. The physics are paradoxical; the engineering is immaculate. *Effect: This device can be used as either a power source or a power sink, decided at the moment of use. As a power source: fully charges any single device or weapon instantly, 3/long rest. As a power sink: absorbs one energy-based attack targeting the user as a reaction, negating the damage entirely and storing it, 2/long rest. Stored energy can be released as a bonus action (same damage type and amount, 30-foot range). Requires attunement.*

17. **Basilisk Protocol Visor** — A tactical visor containing an AI-generated cognitohazardous pattern that, when displayed on the visor's outward-facing micro-LED array, induces temporary neural lock in any biological observer. The pattern exploits fundamental flaws in mammalian visual processing. *Effect: As an action, activate the pattern. All creatures within 30 feet that can see the visor must make a DC 17 Wisdom save or be paralyzed for 1 round (their visual cortex locks up). Creatures that succeed are immune for 24 hours. The user is protected by the visor's inward-facing polarization. 2 uses per long rest. Constructs and creatures without biological vision are immune. Requires attunement.*

18. **Dimensional Pocket Generator** — A belt-mounted device that creates a stable, pocket-sized extradimensional space accessible through a projected aperture. Uses principles the inventor described as "applied Calabi-Yau manifold engineering." *Effect: The user has access to an extradimensional storage space with a volume of approximately 500 cubic feet (roughly a 8x8x8 foot room). Items can be stored and retrieved as a bonus action via a small portal (2-foot diameter) projected from the belt. Living creatures cannot enter. The space has no air, light, or temperature regulation. If the generator is destroyed, all contents are shunted to the Astral Plane. Requires attunement.*

19. **Ghost Frequency Transceiver** — A communication device that transmits on a frequency that does not officially exist — nestled in a gap between quantum electromagnetic modes that conventional physics says carries no energy. Messages sent on this frequency cannot be detected by any known instrument. The inventor's notes simply read: "It works. I don't know why." *Effect: Unlimited range communication on the same plane. Transmissions are completely undetectable and cannot be jammed or intercepted by any means short of divine intervention. Can contact up to 6 attuned receivers. Additionally, once per long rest, the user can broadcast a message to all electronic devices within 1 mile (hijacking screens, speakers, etc.). Requires attunement.*

20. **The Compiler** — A neural implant of unknown origin that grants the user the ability to perceive the underlying "source code" of technological systems. Machines, networks, drones, augmentations — all appear to the user as streams of interpretable data overlaid on physical reality. The implant was not built by any known manufacturer. Its architecture does not match human, corporate, or AI design paradigms. *Effect: The user gains truesight for technological systems: they can see the function, status, vulnerabilities, and connections of any technological device or augmented creature within 60 feet. As an action, the user can "compile" a command into any technological system they can see (DC 18 Technology check), causing it to execute one instruction of the user's choice (shut down, activate, change target, unlock, self-destruct, etc.). 3 uses per long rest. On a critical success, the command is permanent. Requires attunement.*
